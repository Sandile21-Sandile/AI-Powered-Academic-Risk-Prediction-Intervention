About Our Platform

Empowering student success with AI-driven insights at the University of Zululand

Who We Are

AI-Powered Student Academic Risk Prediction System is a passionate team of Computer Science, and MIS Students dedicated to enhancing academic success at the University of Zululand. Our platform leverages artificial intelligence and machine learning to identify students at risk of academic failure early, enabling timely interventions and personalized support.

Our Mission

We aim to transform higher education by enabling data-driven decisions that improve student retention, performance, and overall success. By turning raw student data into actionable insights, we empower educators and institutions to create a supportive and proactive learning environment.
